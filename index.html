Why Hyderabad?

Hyderabad’s central location connects 8 South and Central Indian states, ensuring unparalleled access for educators, policymakers, and innovators. With world-class venues like HICC, seamless global connectivity via Rajiv Gandhi Airport, and alignment with the National Education Policy 2025, the city bridges urban tech advancements with rural education needs, supported by partnerships with the Ministry of Ayush and Fit India.

A thriving EdTech hub, Hyderabad hosts HITEC City, 500+ startups, and premier institutions like IIIT Hyderabad. Its 1,000+ schools and universities draw decision-makers eager to adopt cutting-edge solutions, while cost-effective logistics and a business-friendly environment maximize exhibitor ROI. Hyderabad’s blend of heritage and innovation makes it the ideal stage to shape education’s future.
